Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 21, 2026

This policy currently describes the YD Interactive website and the early Tower-Climber beta. It must be reviewed before advertising, analytics, crash-reporting or account SDKs are activated in a public build.

Who we are

YD Interactive is an independent game developer. Questions about this policy can be sent to ydinteractive.games@gmail.com.

Information you provide

When you contact us for support or feedback, we may receive your email address, message, device information, screenshots, recordings and any other information you choose to include. We use this information to reply, investigate problems and improve the game.

Website hosting

This website is hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information, including IP addresses, for security and service operation. GitHub's own privacy terms govern that processing.

Game data

The early beta may store game progress, settings, scores and virtual currency locally on your device. YD Interactive does not currently operate a player-account system through this website.

Third-party services

Future builds may use third-party services for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, platform achievements or in-app purchases. Before those services are activated in a distributed build, this policy will be updated to identify the relevant providers and explain the data they process.

Data retention

Support messages are retained only as long as reasonably necessary to investigate issues, respond to requests, maintain records and protect the service. You may request deletion of information you previously sent us.

Children

We do not knowingly ask children to send personal information directly to us. A parent or guardian who believes a child has provided personal information may contact us to request its removal.

Your choices

You may contact us to ask what information you have submitted through support, request correction, or request deletion, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

Changes

We may update this policy when the game, website or third-party services change. The latest version will remain available on this page with a revised effective date.
