Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

This policy currently covers the Forever Up website and pre-release development builds. It will be updated before a public beta if analytics, advertising, crash reporting, purchases or other third-party services are activated.

Who we are

Forever Up is developed and published by YD Interactive, an independent game developer.

Privacy questions can be sent to ydinteractive.games@gmail.com .

Information you provide

When you contact YD Interactive for support, beta access or feedback, we may receive information that you choose to provide. This may include your email address, message, device model, operating-system version, screenshots and screen recordings.

This information is used to respond to messages, investigate technical problems and improve Forever Up.

Website hosting

This website is hosted through GitHub Pages. GitHub may process technical information, including IP addresses, for security and service operation. GitHub's own privacy terms govern its processing.

YD Interactive does not currently place its own advertising, tracking cookies or analytics scripts on this website.

Game data

Development versions of Forever Up may store information locally on your device, including:

  • Game progress and best scores
  • Settings and tutorial completion
  • Mission progress
  • Unlocked and equipped cosmetics
  • Sky Shards and Star Cores
  • Claimed progression milestones

This local save data is used to operate the game and preserve progress between sessions.

YD Interactive does not currently operate a player-account system through this website.

Analytics and third-party services

Public beta or release builds may later use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, advertising, beta distribution, platform features or in-app purchases.

Before such services are activated in a distributed build, this policy will be updated to identify the relevant providers and explain the categories of information they process.

Forever Up's analytics setting is intended to remain consent-based.

Data retention

Support and feedback messages may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to respond, investigate problems, improve the game, maintain appropriate records and protect the service.

Local game data remains on the player's device unless it is removed through the game, the operating system or app deletion.

Children's privacy

YD Interactive does not knowingly ask children to submit personal information directly through this website.

A parent or guardian who believes that a child has sent personal information may contact YD Interactive to request its deletion.

Your choices

You may contact YD Interactive to request access to, correction of or deletion of information that you previously submitted through support or feedback communications, subject to applicable legal and operational requirements.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Forever Up, the website or its third-party services change. The latest version will remain available on this page with a revised effective date.
